Dr George Binh Lenon

Speciality: Acupuncture and Chinese Medicine Qualifications: BHlthSc (Hons-ChinMed), RCMP, PhD Dr George Lenon is a founder of GL Natural Healthcare and he is qualified practitioner both in Australia and overseas. He began his Traditional Chinese Medicine journey many years ago. After graduating from Chinese medicine at Victoria University, Dr. George Lenon then went on to earn his PhD at RMIT University with his internationally recognized research in allergies (ie. Hayfever) and weight loss. He is currently lecturing and conducting research at the RMIT university, dedicating his time to his patients after hours at his Strathmore clinic and on the weekends (by appointment only). Dr. Lenon is currently registered with Chinese Medicine Registration Board of Australia. He combines a range of therapies from Tuina massage, Chinese herbal medicine to Acupuncture, providing individualised and high quality care for all his patients.
